What the app does

  • Create and manage personal playlists

  • Search public song metadata and copy YouTube links
  • Background audio, lock screen controls, and CarPlay Now Playing

Common questions

Do I need an account? No account is required on iPhone.

Where is my music stored? On your device. The iOS app does not upload your MP3 library to our servers.

Does the app download YouTube audio? The app plays files you import. Search may open third-party websites if you choose that option; you are responsible for lawful use.

How do I delete my data? Remove tracks in the app or uninstall Noam Music from your device.
