Privacy Policy

Noam Music · Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Noam Music (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use our personal music player on iPhone and on the web. The App is designed for private, personal use. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for advertising.

1. Summary

  • Your music library and MP3 files stay on your device (iOS app).
  • Search queries may be sent to our server and to third-party search services to show song titles and links.
  • No account is required to use the iOS app.
  • Optional cloud backup may be available in the web version only, if configured.

2. Information stored on your device

When you use the App, the following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Playlist names, track titles, artist names, and related metadata
  • Audio files you import (for example MP3 files from the Files app or from a designated app folder on iPhone)
  • Cached album artwork associated with your tracks
  • App preferences stored in local browser or app storage

On the iOS app, this local library data is not uploaded to our servers as part of normal playback or playlist management.

3. Information sent over the network

We may process the following when you use certain features:

  • Search queries — When you search for songs, your search text is sent to our API (hosted on Vercel) and may be forwarded to third-party services that index public YouTube metadata. We use this only to return search results (titles, artists, thumbnails, and public video identifiers). We do not create a marketing profile from your searches.
  • Metadata lookups — When importing a local file, the App may send the file name or similar text to our search API to suggest title and artist information.
  • Artwork URLs — The App may download cover art from third-party URLs (for example YouTube thumbnail servers) and store a cached copy on your device.
  • External downloader link — If you choose the download helper in Search, the App may copy a public YouTube URL to your clipboard and open a third-party website in your browser. We do not receive the content of that download.

4. Optional cloud backup (web version only)

The web version of Noam Music may optionally sync playlists and backup uploaded audio to Google Firebase when cloud credentials are configured. This feature is disabled in the native iOS app. If enabled on web, data is stored under a service account controlled by the developer, not a personal login you create in the App.

5. What we do not collect

  • We do not require your name, email, or phone number to use the iOS app.
  • We do not run in-app advertising or ad tracking.
  • We do not knowingly collect precise location data.
  • We do not sell or rent personal information.

6. Third-party services

The App may interact with services operated by others, including:

  • Vercel — hosts our search API
  • YouTube / Google — public metadata and links shown in search results
  • Piped and similar public APIs — fallback search when our API is unavailable
  • Third-party downloader websites — opened only if you choose that action
  • Apple iOS / CarPlay — system media controls and display of now-playing information
  • Google Firebase — optional cloud backup on web only

Your use of those services is subject to their own terms and privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

7. iOS permissions

  • Files / local storage — used only when you import audio files you select or scan from your app folder.
  • Background audio & CarPlay — used to continue playback and show media controls while driving or when the App is in the background.
  • Network access — used for search and artwork; not required for playing imported files already on your device.

8. Data retention

  • On your device — kept until you delete tracks, playlists, or uninstall the App.
  • Search requests — we do not intentionally maintain long-term logs tied to your identity. Server infrastructure providers may retain short-lived technical logs under their policies.
  • Cloud backup (web) — retained until deleted through that feature or by the developer disabling the service.

9. Your choices and rights

You can:

  • Delete individual tracks or playlists inside the App
  • Remove all local App data by deleting the App from your device
  • Avoid using Search or external downloader features if you do not want network requests
  • Contact us to ask questions about this policy (see Section 12)

Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights. Because the iOS app stores personal content locally and does not require an account, most requests can be fulfilled by deleting data on your device.

10. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical measures appropriate for a personal utility app. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device and the files you import.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
